LostNFoundDog: Reuniting Pets With Their Families

Welcome to LostNFoundDog - the national resource helping reunite lost dogs with their families. Our simple but powerful platform connects lost pet reports with found pet sightings across the country, using location data to quickly match pets with their owners.

1

Register Your Lost Pet

Enter your dog's details including name, breed, distinctive features, and the location where they were last seen. Upload clear photos to help with identification.

2

Set Your Search Radius

Choose how far to search from your pet's last known location. Our system will automatically scan for matches within this area and alert you of potential sightings.

3

Get Instant Matches

Receive immediate notifications when a found dog matches your pet's description. Contact information is provided so you can quickly connect with the person who found your dog.

Key Features

National Database

Access lost and found dog reports from across the entire country in one centralized location.

Smart Matching

Our system uses photo recognition and detailed descriptions to improve match accuracy.

Real-time Alerts

Get instant notifications when a potential match is found in your selected search area.

Community Support

Connect with local volunteers and resources to help expand your search efforts.
